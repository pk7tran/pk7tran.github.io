Peter Tran

A versatile and detail-oriented scientific communicator with years of experience across the medical device, pharmaceuticals, and life sciences spaces. Highly fluent with programs in the Adobe Creative Suite, particularly Photoshop, InDesign, Lightroom, and Illustrator, with additional experience in composing FDA regulatory documents for medical devices. Reliable team player with strong interdisciplinary communication skills, developed through numerous cross-disciplinary collaborations in industry and academia. Additional expertise in biochemistry, synthetic biology, and microbiology.

Completed PhD in Chemical and Biological Engineering at Northwestern University and BSc in Chemical Engineering at Northeastern University.
