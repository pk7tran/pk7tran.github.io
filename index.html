Peter Tran, PhD

Hi! I am a versatile and detail-oriented scientific communicator with over 10 years of experience across the life sciences, medical device, pharmaceutical spaces. I have extensive experience with design platforms such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop and Illustrator), BioRender, and Microsoft Office. I also am a scientific writer, with experience in grants (NIH and NSF), regulatory, and marketing. I thrive at interdisciplinary interfaces, developed through my work and training in academia and industry, with technical expertise in biochemistry, synthetic biology, and microbiology. If you are looking to bring your research to life in a clear and engaging way, please reach out!

I completed PhD in Chemical and Biological Engineering at Northwestern University and BSc in Chemical Engineering at Northeastern University.
