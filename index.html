Peter Tran

A versatile and detail-oriented graphic designer with years of experience in scientific communication and effective figure creation. Highly fluent with programs in the Adobe Creative Suite, particularly Photoshop, InDesign, Lightroom, and Illustrator, with additional experience in composing FDA regulatory documents for medical devices. Reliable team player with strong interdisciplinary communication skills, developed through numerous collaborations across various scientific and medical fields.

Currently a PhD Candidate in Chemical Engineering at Northwestern University.
