Peter Tran, PhD

A versatile and detail-oriented scientific communicator with years of experience across the medical device, pharmaceuticals, and life science spaces. Highly fluent with design platforms such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, InDesign, and Illustrator), BioRender, and Microsoft Office. Additional experience in scientific writing and composing FDA regulatory documents for medical devices. Reliable team player with strong interdisciplinary communication skills, developed through numerous cross-disciplinary collaborations in industry and academia. Additional expertise in biochemistry, synthetic biology, and microbiology.

Completed PhD in Chemical and Biological Engineering at Northwestern University and BSc in Chemical Engineering at Northeastern University.
